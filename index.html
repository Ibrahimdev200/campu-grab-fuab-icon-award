How The Awards Work

1. Nominations (Free)

Nominate colleagues or yourself. It's completely free! The top 2 most-nominated candidates in each category are chosen as finalists.

2. Finalist Stage (₦5,000)

Every student selected as a finalist receives an immediate cash reward of ₦5,000. There are exactly 2 finalists per category.

3. Final Voting (₦50,000)

Voting opens on the finalists, merging gender categories (leaving 4 finalists in Best Vibe and 4 in Rising Star). Cast your vote to crown the category winner (₦50,000 cash prize)!
